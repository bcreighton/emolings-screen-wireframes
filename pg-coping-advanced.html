STILL NO PROGRESS?

There are times where you won't be able to move forward; that's okay. It isn't a failure, it's just a part of the process. This can happen more often than not if your child is not formulating words yet. If this is the case, take a break, let your child feel what they're feeling and revisit it at a later time when they are not working through this emotion/feeling.

That being said, there is also a chance that you've gone down the wrong path. If you've been coping with the incorrect emotion/feeling use the button below to start over.
